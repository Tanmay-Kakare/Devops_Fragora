How To Use Our Signature Perfumes

Maximize the beauty and longevity of your fragrance with these simple application steps.

Spray on Pulse Points
Apply perfume on neck, wrists, and behind ears for a lasting impression.
Avoid Rubbing
Let the fragrance settle naturally. Rubbing can dull the top notes.
Reapply as Needed
For longer days, carry a mini spray to refresh the scent mid-day.
