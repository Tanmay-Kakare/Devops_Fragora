How to Use Fragora Perfumes

Tips to make your fragrance last longer and smell even better

Step 1: Spray on Pulse Points

Apply on wrists, neck, and behind the ears for better projection.

Step 2: Apply After Shower

Clean, hydrated skin holds scents longer. Pat dry and spray.

Step 3: Store Properly

Keep your bottles away from heat and sunlight to preserve aroma.
